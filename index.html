About

Why we exist

Bees all over the world are disappearing due to pesticides, parasites spreading disease and habitat loss.

Our mission

We want to raise awareness about the importance of bees and the creation of honey in cities. Using a model similar to that already implemented in New York, we strive to set up Bee Hives on London rooftops throughout the city!
